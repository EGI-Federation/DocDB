Registering a Certificate

You must register your certificate with the LARP document database before you will have full access.

  1. In a browser that has had your certificate imported, go to the LARP document database page: http://larpdocs.fnal.gov/
    Or go straight to the secure page and skip the next click.
  2. Click "Private LARP documents"
  3. If you get a window "choose a digital certificate", pick the desired certificate. Click "OK"
  4. You'll get a message "You have presented a valid certificate, but are not yet authorized to access the Docdb." Click on the link just below the message "Apply for?" This is to register your certificate in the database.
  5. A form will appear - enter your email and highight the desired access permission(s) and then "submit." There is a comments box which you can use to swear at the system.
  6. Someone should authorize you within a few hours during working hours. Send email to larpdb-admin@fanl.gov if you don't hear anything within a day.

People are investigating ways to streamline this process. We are not yet sure how much is possible.
